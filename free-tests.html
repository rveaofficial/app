JEE Mains Instructions

Please read the following instructions carefully before starting the exam:

  • The total duration of the test is 180 minutes.
  • There are three sections: Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.
  • Each section contains 20 multiple-choice questions.
  • You will earn 4 marks for each correct answer and lose 1 mark for each incorrect answer.
  • You can navigate between sections at any time during the test.
  • Please ensure you have a stable internet connection throughout the test.
  • Once the timer ends, the test will be automatically submitted.
  • Do not refresh or close the browser window during the test.
  • Click the "Start Test" button to begin.
