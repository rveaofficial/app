Test Instructions

  • This test consists of 30 multiple-choice questions. Each question has four options, but only one is correct.
  • For every correct answer, you will earn 4 points.
  • For every wrong answer, 1 point will be deducted.
  • You can skip a question, and no points will be added or deducted for unanswered questions.
  • Make sure to review all your answers before submitting the test.
  • The timer will start as soon as you click the "Start Test" button, and you will have a limited amount of time to complete the test.
  • Once you have completed the test, your score and a summary of correct and incorrect answers will be displayed.

Note: Ensure a stable internet connection before starting the test.
